# About FlyerBoard

## Our Story

FlyerBoard was born in 2025 from a simple frustration: existing classified platforms weren't doing enough to protect Australians from scams and fraud. As someone who experienced the anxiety of wondering whether a buyer or seller was legitimate, I knew there had to be a better way.

Based in Melbourne and serving communities across Australia, FlyerBoard is built on the belief that buying and selling second-hand goods should be **safe, simple, and trustworthy**.

## Our Mission

We're creating a safe, easy-to-use, local community platform where Australians can buy and sell with confidence. Every feature we build is designed with one question in mind: *Does this make our community safer?*
